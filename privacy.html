Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Toby Chan ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you subscribe to Toby’s Life Updates SMS Program.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your phone number when you voluntarily opt-in to receive SMS updates from us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number solely to send you the life updates, project announcements, and personal messages you requested. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with our SMS service provider (Twilio) solely to deliver messages to you.

4. Your Rights

You may opt-out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also request to have your information deleted by contacting us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact Toby Chan.
